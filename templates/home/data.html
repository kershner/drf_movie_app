About the Data...

This app uses free, open source data from The Movie Database.

Since this is just a toy/exploratory app, the methodology used for pulling data wasn't as straightforward as simply pulling every single object from TMDB's database, which would take quite a bit of time and storage.

I started with 1000 movies from the /popular TMDB endpoint, and crawled through each movie's cast to begin populating people.

I then needed to populate people's filmographies, but pulling every single movie for every single person seemed a bit extreme, given my time constraints. I ended up sorting my list of people by popularity, a handy attribute available from the TMDB API, and pulling filmographies for the top 1000 most popular people in my database.
